## Step 3: Market Size Check (20 minutes)

For your top 3 candidates, estimate:

### Total Addressable Market (TAM)
How many potential customers exist?

**Example:** Email sentiment analyzer
- SaaS companies with support teams: ~50,000 globally
- Small businesses with customer service: ~500,000
- TAM: 550,000 potential customers

**Formula:** Be conservative. 10,000+ potential customers = viable market.

### Willingness to Pay
Search these places to gauge demand:
- Reddit (search for your problem + "recommendation", "tool", "software")
- Twitter (search keywords + "hate", "frustrating", "need")
- Indie Hackers (search for similar products)

**Red flag:** If nobody is complaining about this problem online, demand may not exist.

**Green flag:** Active threads with 50+ replies = proven demand.
